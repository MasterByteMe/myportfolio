About Me

Hi, I'm Mark Anthony — a self-taught Full Stack Developer driven by curiosity, creativity, and a genuine passion for building things that work beautifully. My journey into tech began with Udemy courses and YouTube tutorials, and over time, I’ve gained strong hands-on experience in both front-end and back-end technologies. Before transitioning into web development, I worked for eight years as an Admin/Receiver at Alshaya Group in Doha, Qatar, and also gained valuable experience in customer service. These roles taught me the importance of organization, communication, and delivering results — skills I now bring to every development project I undertake. As a career shifter, I’m eager to prove myself in the tech industry. I specialize in creating seamless user experiences, responsive layouts, and clean, maintainable code. Whether you're a business looking to build your online presence, a startup with a bold idea, or simply need help improving your existing site — I’m here to help bring your vision to life through thoughtful, functional web applications.
